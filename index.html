WHY CHOOSE US?

  • We provide biking trails within a 160 km radius from major South African cities with plans to expand to more cities. This extensive coverage ensures that users can easily find biking trails no matter where they are in South Africa
  • While some information may be user-generated and thus subject to inaccuracies, we encourage users to contribute by suggesting new trails or providing updates. This collaborative approach ensures that the app's database stays up-to-date and relevant
  • We offer detailed information about biking trails, including distance, difficulty, elevation, and features. Users can make informed decisions about which trails to explore based on their preferences and skill levels.
  • The app provides map routes for biking trails whenever available, allowing users to visualize their ride before hitting the trail. This feature enhances the user experience and helps cyclists navigate unfamiliar terrain with confidence
  • Pedal Buddy is constantly evolving to address the challenge of obtaining comprehensive information about biking trails in South Africa. With regular updates and new features in the pipeline, users can expect continuous improvement and enhancement of their biking experience.
  • Your Safety Is Our Priority. We try our best to ensure that your data is safe.
